About TEDxLGS JT

TEDxLGS JT is a student-led initiative, independently organized under the license of TED. We aim to bring together curious minds to share ideas worth spreading, sparking deep discussion and connection within the LGS community.

Our event celebrates creativity, leadership, and innovation, providing a platform for local voices to share their unique perspectives on global challenges and inspiring futures.

Join us on November 3rd for an unforgettable experience filled with mind-blowing talks, networking opportunities, and a day dedicated to new perspectives and enlightenment.

  • Inspiring talks from local leaders and innovators.
  • Interactive sessions and workshops.
  • Networking with passionate community members.
